Account Security FAQ

How do I keep my account secure?

To keep your account secure:

  • Use a strong, unique password with at least 8 characters
  • Never share your password with anyone
  • Change your password regularly
  • Log out after using public computers
  • Enable notification preferences to stay informed about account activity
What if I forget my password?

You can reset your password by:

  1. Going to the login page
  2. Clicking "Forgot Password"
  3. Entering your email/phone and role
  4. Verifying with OTP sent to your contact
  5. Setting a new password
Can I change my email or phone number?

Currently, your contact email/phone is linked to your account role. To update it, please contact our support team through the Support section.

How is my data protected?

We protect your data by:

  • Encrypting passwords using industry-standard hashing
  • Using secure OTP verification for account actions
  • Storing personal data securely
  • Not sharing your information with third parties
What happens when I delete my account?

When you delete your account:

  • All your profile information is permanently removed
  • Your bookings and tickets are deleted
  • Your event history is removed
  • All support tickets are closed
  • This action cannot be undone
